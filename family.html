Family

My family includes my mom, my dad, my brother, and I. We are from Caracas, Venezuela and we moved to San Diego in 2006. My brother's name is Moises, he is 23 years old, and has been living in Seattle since he graduated from Gonzaga in 2022.

We have been living in San Diego ever since we moved to the United States. We live in Poway which is in the Northeast area of San Diego County, just 15 minutes away from the beach and 30 minutes away from downtown.

As a family, we love watching sports especially basketball and soccer. We also like to travel. Some of the places we have traveled to are London, France, Spain, Italy, Sweden, Australia, Cananda, Mexico, and a couple of states in the United States. My parents like to go play tennis against each other at our neighborhood park. We also love going to the beach and going on hikes.
